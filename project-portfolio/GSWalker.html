G & S Walker Beach House

Location Matarangi Beach Coromandel
Architect Matt Chaplin, Sumich Architects - (09) 522-1286

Project Details

House of the Year 2002 Supreme Award Winner

This beachfront house sits in a unique wooded setting at the edge of popular Coromandel Peninsula. The house is designed within the context of this large green site. The clientâ€™s requested a holiday dwelling that created a relaxed and secure indoor/outdoor living environment and to meet the needs of an extended family life. The 4 forms of the dwelling take their shape from typical rural structures and are placed to create numerous sheltered living spaces between and around them. Entry is through a cedar gate in a high bagged plaster wall opening into a tree filled green courtyard. All the forms open into this sheltered courtyard. The plan is only one room deep at any point allowing a visual connection between the courtyards and beach beyond. Materials are restrained in palette and have been selected for robustness. Bandsawn weatherboards, timber battens and TG&V reflect the beach environment and are typical elements of the traditional NZ bach. The weatherboards have been painted dark in colour to give strength to the forms and contrast with the white joinery and soft grey of the zinc corrugate roof. Cedar battens have been used to create sheltered exterior spaces and to control the elements. The effect of these is to filter sun and light, creating spaces that are diffuse, light and breezy. Clean exterior detailing is reflected in the interior to create an overall ambience of a relaxed holiday environment.


Commenced 05 February 2000
Completed 01 December 2001

Client Referral - Gavin and Susan Walker

Rod Percival built our beach house at 770 Matarangi Drive, Matarangi during the course of 2001. We have no hesitation in recommending the firm for other building projects.

Builders, contractors, architect and owners collaborated happily on the project and reached the end on very good terms. This is due in no small part to the "let's get it right" attitude of Rod and his team. Any issues which arose during the course of the building were resolved quickly, amicably and fairly, and this attitude has continued after the completion of the project. Any small problems are fixed promptly and efficiently.

We were also very happy with the exremely efficient administration and good communication of Rod's office staff. All changes, additions and deletions to the contract were dealt with in a way that ensured we knew what was happening and why. We were exremely impressed with the extras we received; a folder containing all manuals, brochures and guarantees which had been assembled during the course of the project, and a photograph album which traced the progress of our house. We were also grateful for the digital photos we received on a regular basis by email.

It was important for us that our house was finished in time for us to enjoy the summer at the beach, and Rod himself worked very hard to achieve that. All hands were on deck for some very long hours of work immediately prior to Christmas, and all was ready on time.

Rod had an excellent team of builders on this project, and almost all of his contractors were of a very high standard. The few problems experienced - getting the painting finished being the major one - Rod worked very proactively to solve. The contractors have all shown themselves very willing to provide continuing service, which was an important feature for us.

We are thrilled with our finished house. Having worked with a number of builders in Australia and Auckland, we rank Rod Percival Builders with the very best.

Yours faithfull,
Gavin and Susan Walker
