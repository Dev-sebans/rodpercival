I & L Roby Beach House

Location Hot Water Beach Coromandel Peninsula
Architect Linda Vugler Concept Drawings - Rod Percival material selection and Design

Project Details

This stunning house commenced in February 2004 and has commanding views out to the Mercury Islands. Built from Hot Blocs with plaster exterior it also features imported Italian Roof Tiles and Smartwood Aluminium Joinery. The house contains 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and has been designed to follow the contour of the land.

Commenced 03 January 2004
Completed 07 May 2006

Rod's Comment

From the moment Lynda and I met Ian & Lorraine on site I knew this was to be an amazing build and being allowed to contribute my ideas and passion into the design with Lynda was the ultimate, the results were incredible!
