Wright Beach House

Location Matarangi Beach Coromandel Peninsula
Architect Architects Patterson

Project Details

Use it for Christmas

Commenced 08 July 2000
Completed 23 December 2000

Rod's Comment

Zinc exterior cladding was imported from France and didn't arrive until three weeks before completion date. To enable us to continue working we "wrapped" the exterior and had the interior almost completed before the zinc arrived. Luckily the weather held for us. David and Angela were so generous in their praise for making it happen by Christmas.
