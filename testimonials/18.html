John Sheriff

03.09.07

Congratulations on achieving this Home which I know is something that is dear to you and is something that people dream of and do nothing about. It is pleasing to see that you have addressed this and have produced a Home that has been judged by your peers, and has won the Highest Award for this Category in the Auckland area. To win this building an Eco Green house is no mean feat and we wish you well in the National Competition.

As an ex Judge in the National Competition my eyes were everywhere and want to congratulate you and your team on producing a product that is excellent in every way, the standard of workmanship is of the highest standard and your team have achieved perfection in every way.

Again thanks for being able to share in your success and look forward to seeing more in the future.

John Sheriff
