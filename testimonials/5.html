Testimonials from New Zealand Australia & Europe

Duff & Julie Oliver

12 August 1999


Dear Rod,

Julie and I would like to thank you for the designing and building of our new home which we have been occupying since 1st April 1999.

From our first meeting in August last year, it has been a pleasure working with you, your staff and sub-contractors.

We have had a number of visitors who have a lot of building expertise and their comments are very complimentary of the pleasing design and high quality of workmanship.

Most of all Julie and I are thoroughly enjoying living in the home.

We would highly recommend your Company to future clients and would be happy to show them through our home.

Thank you both for making building our new home such a happy and enjoyable experience.

regards,

Duff Oliver
